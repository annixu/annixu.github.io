Explore Boston Airbnb listings with $100 or less

What can you get with less than $100 budget to spend on Airbnb in Boston? This is a beesworm data visualization of Airbnb listings in different neightborhoods of Boston by Bed and Bath types. Each circle represents an Airbnb listing that are color-coded by the room types, and contains information like the URL, price, accommodation, and amenities.

The dashboard can be used as a exploration tool for low-budget listings in Boston, and as a visualization to see listing patterns among different neighborhoods. The data used is taken from the Boston Airbnb Open Data and might include outdated links for some listings urls.
