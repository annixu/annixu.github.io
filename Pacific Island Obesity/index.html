The Fattest Lands on Earth

The Pacific islands are home to nine of the top 10 countries for obesity globally. Rates of obesity range from 35% to 50% in the region and one in five children are estimated to be obese.

People are amputated, hospitalized and dying from complications of obesity related diseases, largely due to the adoption of imported Western food and and their eating habbits. Perception for Western food, affordability, and taste are key factors that indulge people and the younger generations to eat their health away.

This project explores transcripts data of Youtube videos that discusses obesity in Pacific Islands. I extracted keywords related to the phenomenon hoping to tell the story through contexts of words.
